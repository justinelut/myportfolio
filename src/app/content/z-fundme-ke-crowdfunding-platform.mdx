# Fundme.co.ke – A Donation-Based Crowdfunding Platform for Kenyans

## Project Overview

Fundme.co.ke is a donation-based crowdfunding platform aimed at transforming how Kenyans raise funds for their causes. Through a modern design and intuitive user experience, the platform provides an easy-to-use tool for individuals and organizations to seek support and achieve their fundraising goals.
